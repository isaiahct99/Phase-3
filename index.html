ABOUT OUR PROGRAM

Basic computer and technology literacy classes with a hands on approach for websites, digital menus, and overall digital and tech literacy training. These classes create a welcoming and creative environment for small business owners who are not well equipped or have little to no knowledge about technology and digital literacy.

OUR MISSION

The Southeast Cities Technology Collaborative, under the management of the Southeast Community Development Corporation, will conduct a three year technology initiative designed to: Expand community broadband access points Provide computer literacy training Enhance existing community service programs throughout the Southeast region of Los Angeles County.

    LESSON TOPICS

  • Professional websites
  • Staff training on digital literacy skills
  • Digital Menus

Frequently Asked Questions

  • DO I NEED MY OWN COMPUTER?

    • No all equipment will be provided for anyone attending the class.

  • HOW MUCH DOES THE PROGRAM COST?

    • This program is entirely free. Paid for by our amazing sponsors and community donations.

  • WHO IS THIS PROGRAM FOR?

    • Anyone starting or currently having a small business and is in need of Digital and Technology training to better equip your business. Also anyone looking to improve their knowledge and abilities with digital literacy.
